Here is a collection of cards and posters I've done lately. I love to draw guitars and other musical instruments. When I was young, I used to draw fictional album covers. Nowadays I search inspiration also from comic books, movie posters, magazines, architecture and other artists works.

My favourite phase is planning composition and doing background studies to get all facts right. Sometimes planning takes several weeks, after that all elements are figured out and the actual drawing can take place. I use pencils, Copic Multiliner SP pens, black ink and watercolors.
